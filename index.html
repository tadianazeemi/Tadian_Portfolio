About Me

Where Creativity
Meets Precision

I'm Tadian Ahmad Azeemi, a graphic designer who transforms ideas into powerful visual identities. With a sharp eye for detail and a passion for clean, impactful design, I craft logos, brand systems, print collateral, and motion graphics that leave a lasting impression.

My primary toolkit is Adobe Illustrator for all vector design work — from minimalist marks to complex mascot logos — and After Effects for bringing logos to life through smooth, cinematic animations.
